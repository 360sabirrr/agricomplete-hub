Decision Matrix

Weather rulebook

Rain or storm

Pause irrigation, pesticide spray, and top dressing. Focus on drainage and runoff control.

Wind above 15 km/h

Hold pesticides and foliar fertilizers. Use low-drift nozzles only after wind settles.

Heat above 32 C

Irrigate before 8 AM. Avoid pesticide spray and split fertilizer to prevent stress.

High humidity

Watch fungal pressure, improve airflow, and avoid overhead watering near evening.
